About Us

World in Need Baptist Church – Congo is a mission-driven ministry dedicated to reaching villages across the Democratic Republic of the Congo with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
Our work is centered on a saturation approach—bringing the message of salvation to every village, discipling new believers, and training faithful men and women to become pastors, missionaries, and church leaders.
Through Bible studies, village outreaches, children’s ministries, and leadership training, we are seeing lives transformed and churches planted in places where the Gospel has not yet reached.
We believe that every soul matters, every village deserves the Gospel, and every believer can be equipped to serve.
